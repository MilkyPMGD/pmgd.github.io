Bakerite

Design and marketing
Read: 2 minutes


A provider and supplier of 'Free from' specialty baking ingredients. They are an experienced importer and distributor of high-quality goods for the baking industry that put quality above everything else. I was responsible for producing a handful of monthly email designs, plus accompaning social media posts, to promote their unique range of specialty ingredients.

My responsibilities would streth to producing templated collateral for in business, and potentially external, use. To best present their range of specialty ingredients, an interactive PowerPoint template was produced for the client.

Having this in a templated format meant that, as the client's range of ingredients expanded, the content could easily be updated in a semi-modular format ensuring that the template's use would last for as many uses as necessary.
